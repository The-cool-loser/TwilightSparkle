Twilight Sparkle, Princess of Friendship

About Twilight

Her Start

Twilight Sparkle started off as a nerd with a preference for studying over making friends, but her teacher, Princess Celestia, forced her to go to Ponyville and go on an adventure to stop her sister from overthrowing her in order to make the night last forever. In that adventure she made 5 friends, which made her opt to stay in Ponyville.

Becoming a Princess

Eventually, by exploring the depth of friendship, magic and the connection between the two, she was promoted to princess.

Starting a school

As part of her royal duties of spreading friendships and positivity over the world, she decided to make a school to teach the 6 main principles of friendship. Her school had a rough start that forced her to bend a few of the rules set by the EEA (Equestria's Education Administration).
